Stochastic Model of Lambda Phage Infection (Lysogeny)

Description



Control of the outcome of phage lambda infection is one of the best understood regulatory systems. Thieffry and Thomas proposed a logical model to describe the dynamics of the network, which is a bistable switch between lysis and lysogeny. Lysogeny is the state where the phage will incorporate its DNA into the bacterium and become dormant. In other words, if the phage is in lysogeny, then the gene CI is fully expressed and all other genes are off.
